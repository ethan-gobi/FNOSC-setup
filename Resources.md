| Service | Number | How to Reach | Availability |
|---|---|---|---|
| NORS Safer Consumption Hotline | 1-888-688-6677 | Call or Text | 24/7, peer-run, harm reduction |
| Crisis Text Line (Indigenous) | 686868 (youth) / 741741 (adults) | Text FIRST NATIONS, INUIT, or METIS | 24/7 |
| 9-8-8 Suicide Crisis Helpline | 988 | Call or Text | 24/7, English & French |
| Wellness Together Canada | 741741 (adults) / 686868 (youth) | Text WELLNESS | 24/7, mental health + substance use |
